about me

Hair Stylist/Makeup Artist

Meet Prerana Sarkar, a talented hair stylist and makeup artist with a passion for helping her clients look and feel their best.
My interest in the beauty industry began at a young age, and since then, I have honed my skills through extensive training and experience.
My approach to hair styling and makeup artistry is both creative and collaborative. I take the time to understand my clients' individual needs and preferences and work closely with them to create a look that is tailored to their unique style and personality.
My unwavering focus on even the smallest details and unwavering dedication to delivering the best possible results ensures that each client feels confident and beautiful when leaving my care.
